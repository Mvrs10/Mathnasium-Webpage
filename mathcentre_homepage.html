FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Answers to common questions to resolve basic outstanding concerns
Do you specialize exclusively in mathematics?
Yes, we do. Mathnasium was established in 2002 and now operates over 1,000 Learning Centres in North America, providing the best personalized math tutoring nearby.
Will my child receive face-to-face instruction?
Yes! Not only does your child get the best personalized math tutoring nearby, but they also receive face-to-face instruction.
How do you help students?
Mathnasium uses modern techniques and fun methods to help children fall in love with math instead of getting bored or terrified by lessons. We tailor our instruction to your child’s learning style and needs.
Who develops your curriculum?
Mathnasium’s proprietary curriculum was created by qualified teachers and curriculum consultants whose goal was to make learning easy and approachable for students.
Will you give my child homework?
No! We don’t burden students with extra homework. Adding homework would risk frustration and overload.
